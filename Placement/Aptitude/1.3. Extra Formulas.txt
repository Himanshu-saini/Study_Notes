===================
Important Formulas
===================
- Sum of First N natural Numbers
  ------------------------------
  - S = N*(N+1)/2

- Sum of Square of First N natural Numbers 
  -----------------------------------------
  - S = [N*(N+1)*(2N+1)] / 6

- Sum of the cube of first N natural numbers
  ------------------------------------------
  - S = [ [N*(N+1)]/2 ]^2
