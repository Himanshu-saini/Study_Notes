===================
Elements of Design
===================
- Building material for design
  
1. Space
-----------
- Empty area on design
- Negatiave space - empty
- Positive space - Filled space 

2. Lines
----------
- Primal element
- Straigt, surved, or braked

- Thick diagonal line : excitement , emergency 
- Thick verticle line : stability

3. Shapes
-----------
- Organic - found in nature 
- Inorganic - mammade
- Abstact

4. Size
---------
- scale or mass 
- draws attention to larger element 
- relative to other elements 

5. Texture
--------------
- physical or visual
- repeating shapes continuausly
- create sensse of depth

6. Value
--------
- Tones of light and dark
- Emphasis of certain aspects

- Check on Gray scale 
  - used to check the contrast
  - 

7. Color
---------
- psycological effect
- Good design works in the absesnce of color

- White
  - Symbol of purity

- Group elements together
